# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts.
- Think before posting any content online.
- Use strong privacy settings to protect your personal information.
- Verify information before sharing it to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Build a positive digital presence that reflects your values and goals.

## Don’ts
- Do not share sensitive personal information like passwords or bank details.
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online harassment.
- Avoid accepting requests or messages from unknown or suspicious accounts.
- Do not overshare personal details that could affect your safety or reputation.
