# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Always verify the sender before clicking on any link or downloading attachments.
- Do not share sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, or bank details with anyone.
- Check the URL of websites carefully to ensure they are genuine (look for HTTPS and correct spelling).
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like email and banking apps.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions or accessing sensitive accounts.
- Regularly update your passwords and use strong, unique passwords for different accounts.
- For UPI safety: Never approve unknown payment requests or “collect requests” from strangers.
- For UPI safety: Do not scan random QR codes or click suspicious payment links.
- Install apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store.
- If you suspect fraud, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline number **1930**.
